Completely written in ES6, Garden.js is designed to be thin and easy to understand.

Designed for quick and dirty prototyping of ideas. Use your own own

I want to forget learning a 3D framework or designing an architecture for new ideas. I know I need a 'Box' but I don't want to think about tessellating polypoints. I know math but I don't care about light vector scattering or setting up shaded splines!?

--- Do you want to do 3D apps and games, but don't give a shit about Fresnels, emmisive textures, vertex shading, octrees or other techy sounding wizardry?

Do you want to press Go, and worry about collisions, accelerated loading, post-processing and scene-picking tomorrow?

Plucking all the cool ideas off the interweb we have Garden.js full of the best ideas from a 3D world. An attempt to delete the complexities of 3D, so you can focus on whatever the hell you're building.

Garden.js is a 3D prototyping engine. Abstracting the complex boilerplate into human readable 3D stuff.

We've done our best to provide all the best parts of the best 3D frameworks - so when you need Reflection-probes? we've got you covered.
